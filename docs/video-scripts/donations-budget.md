# Video Walkthrough: Donations + Budget
Duration: ~4 minutes
Feature: Donation Logging (#11), Budget Tracker

## Script

### Scene 1: Opening (0:00 - 0:15)
SCREEN: Dashboard
VOICE: "Campaign finance compliance is non-negotiable. Ontario law requires you to track every dollar in and every dollar out. Poll City makes it painless."

### Scene 2: Recording a Donation (0:15 - 0:50)
SCREEN: Click Donations in sidebar
VOICE: "Go to Donations. Click Record Donation. Enter the donor name, amount, and method — cash, cheque, e-transfer, or credit card. Ontario municipal limit is $1,200 per donor."
ACTION: Fill in donation form
VOICE: "If the donor is already a contact, link them. If not, Poll City creates a new contact automatically. Every donation over $25 must be receipted — Poll City tracks this."

### Scene 3: Quick Capture (0:50 - 1:15)
SCREEN: Quick Capture button
VOICE: "At a fundraiser, use Quick Capture. Name, amount, method. Three fields, one tap. Log 20 donations in 10 minutes."

### Scene 4: Donation List (1:15 - 1:40)
SCREEN: Donation list with filters
VOICE: "Your donation list shows every contribution — amount, donor, date, status, method. Filter by status: pledged, received, receipted. Search by donor name."

### Scene 5: Budget Overview (1:40 - 2:15)
SCREEN: Click Budget in sidebar
VOICE: "The Budget page shows your spending limit — set by Ontario election law based on your municipality. You see allocations on top, expenses below. The progress bar shows how much of your limit you have used."
ACTION: Point out spending limit, used amount, remaining

### Scene 6: Adding an Expense (2:15 - 2:45)
SCREEN: Add Expense
VOICE: "Record every expense. Category, vendor, amount, payment method. Upload the receipt — take a photo right from your phone. Every expense needs a receipt for your financial filing."
ACTION: Add an expense with receipt

### Scene 7: Budget Rules and Alerts (2:45 - 3:10)
SCREEN: Budget Rules
VOICE: "Set budget rules — alert me when printing exceeds $2,000, or when total spending hits 80% of the limit. Poll City warns you before you overspend."

### Scene 8: Financial Compliance (3:10 - 3:40)
SCREEN: Compliance section
VOICE: "Ontario requires financial filing 90 days after election day. Poll City exports everything you need — donor list with amounts, expense list with receipts, compliance summary. One click, filing-ready."

### Scene 9: Closing (3:40 - 4:00)
SCREEN: Dashboard with finance stats
VOICE: "Track donations weekly, not at the deadline. Your CFO will love you. And remember — ask early, ask often, ask specifically. '$50 by Friday' works better than 'please donate.'"

## Verification Checklist
- [ ] Donation recording works (all methods)
- [ ] Quick capture creates donation + contact
- [ ] Donation list loads with filters
- [ ] Budget overview shows spending limit
- [ ] Expense creation with receipt upload works
- [ ] Budget rules create and alert
- [ ] Export produces compliance-ready CSV
- [ ] $1,200 limit warning shown when exceeded
