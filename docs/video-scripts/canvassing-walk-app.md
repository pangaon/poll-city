# Video Walkthrough: Canvassing + Walk App
Duration: ~5 minutes
Feature: Turf Builder (#6), Walk App + GPS (#7)

## Script

### Scene 1: Opening (0:00 - 0:15)
SCREEN: Dashboard
VOICE: "Canvassing wins elections. Period. Poll City gives you a complete system — build turfs, assign volunteers, and send them out with a mobile walk list that logs every door. Here is how it works."

### Scene 2: Creating a Turf (0:15 - 1:00)
SCREEN: Click Canvassing in sidebar
VOICE: "Go to Canvassing. You will see your turfs — the areas you have divided your riding into."
ACTION: Click "Create Turf"
VOICE: "Click Create Turf. Draw the boundary on the map by clicking corner points. Give it a name — something your volunteers will recognise, like 'Oak Street North' or 'Poll 42 West'. Aim for 80-100 doors per turf — that is a comfortable 2-3 hour canvass shift."
ACTION: Draw a polygon on the map
ACTION: Name the turf
VOICE: "Hit Save. Poll City counts the contacts inside your turf boundary automatically."

### Scene 3: Assigning Volunteers (1:00 - 1:30)
SCREEN: Turf detail page
VOICE: "Open a turf and click Assign Volunteer. Pick someone from your team. They will see this turf in their Walk List when they open the app on their phone."
ACTION: Assign a volunteer
VOICE: "You can also assign a volunteer group — send your whole Saturday team to a turf."

### Scene 4: Creating a Canvass Script (1:30 - 2:00)
SCREEN: Canvassing Scripts section
VOICE: "Before your team goes out, create a canvass script. Click New Script. Write an opening line, your key messages — keep it to 3 — and your closing ask. Poll City has templates for supporter, persuadable, and opposition scripts."
ACTION: Show script creation form
VOICE: "Your volunteers will see this script on their phone while they are at the door."

### Scene 5: The Walk List — Mobile (2:00 - 3:00)
SCREEN: Mobile phone view of /canvassing/walk
VOICE: "Now the magic. Your canvasser opens Poll City on their phone and goes to Walk List. They see a card for each door in their assigned turf, sorted by street for efficient walking."
ACTION: Show a contact card
VOICE: "Each card shows the person's name, address, and their current support level. The canvasser knocks the door, has their conversation, then taps the result."
ACTION: Tap a support level button
VOICE: "Supporter, leaning, undecided, against, or not home. One tap. Done. The result saves immediately and syncs to the campaign database."
ACTION: Show the result saving
VOICE: "If they want to add a note — 'concerned about hydro rates, has a dog, best time is evening' — they type it right there."

### Scene 6: Not Home and Revisits (3:00 - 3:30)
SCREEN: Not home card
VOICE: "Not home? Mark it. The card moves to the bottom of the list. Come back later — your best conversion rate is on the second and third visit. Poll City tracks how many times each door has been attempted."

### Scene 7: Turf Progress (3:30 - 4:00)
SCREEN: Turf list with completion percentages
VOICE: "Back in the office, you can see every turf's completion percentage. Oak Street North — 78% done. Poll 42 West — 45%. You know exactly where to send people next."
ACTION: Show the leaderboard
VOICE: "The leaderboard shows your top canvassers. Sarah knocked 47 doors tonight. Victor is at 348 total. Celebrate these people — they are winning your election."

### Scene 8: Route Optimization (4:00 - 4:20)
SCREEN: Turf map with route
VOICE: "Click Optimize Route on any turf. Poll City calculates the most efficient walking order — less crossing streets, more doors per hour. Your volunteers will thank you."

### Scene 9: Closing (4:20 - 5:00)
SCREEN: Dashboard with canvassing stats
VOICE: "Every door knocked feeds your support numbers, your GOTV list, and your analytics. The campaign that knocks the most doors wins. Get your turfs built, get your team assigned, and get out there. The doors are not going to knock themselves."
