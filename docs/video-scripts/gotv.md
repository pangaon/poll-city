# Video Walkthrough: GOTV (Get Out The Vote)
Duration: ~4 minutes
Feature: GOTV Priority + Upload (#8)

## Script

### Scene 1: Opening (0:00 - 0:15)
SCREEN: Dashboard
VOICE: "GOTV is the single most important thing you do in the final 10 days. Every confirmed supporter who does not vote is a vote you left on the table. Poll City's GOTV system makes sure that does not happen."

### Scene 2: The Priority List (0:15 - 0:50)
SCREEN: Click GOTV in sidebar, Priority List tab
VOICE: "Go to GOTV. The Priority List shows every contact ranked by how likely they are to vote for you. P1 is your strongest supporters who always vote — call them on election morning. P2 is likely supporters — call them the day before. P3 is persuadable — call them 3 days out. P4 is low priority — spend zero resources here on election day."
ACTION: Show the tiers with counts

### Scene 3: Uploading a Voted List (0:50 - 1:30)
SCREEN: Upload Voted tab
VOICE: "On election day and during advance voting, the clerk provides a voted list — everyone who has cast a ballot. Upload it here. Drag your CSV or Excel file in. Poll City matches names and addresses automatically."
ACTION: Upload a file
VOICE: "Within seconds you see how many matched. 47 of your supporters have voted. 23 have not. Those 23 are your priority right now — call them."

### Scene 4: Strike-Off (1:30 - 2:00)
SCREEN: Strike Off tab
VOICE: "The Strike Off view shows your supporters in real time. Green means voted. Red means not yet. As you upload new voted lists throughout the day, this updates live. Your phone bankers should be calling everyone in red."
ACTION: Show the struck-off list updating

### Scene 5: Election Day Command (2:00 - 2:45)
SCREEN: Election Day Command tab
VOICE: "The Election Day Command centre is your war room. You see hourly turnout pacing, how many supporters have voted vs your target, and which polls are lagging. If Poll 42 is at 30% turnout at 2pm and your target is 60%, you know where to send your next volunteer."
ACTION: Show pacing metrics and poll breakdown

### Scene 6: GOTV Templates (2:45 - 3:15)
SCREEN: Templates section
VOICE: "Create GOTV call scripts. A P1 script is simple — 'Hi, polls close at 8pm, your polling station is St. James Church on Oak Street.' A P3 script needs your pitch. Save these and your phone bankers read them while calling."

### Scene 7: Closing (3:15 - 4:00)
SCREEN: Dashboard with GOTV stats
VOICE: "Election day math is simple. Know your number — how many votes you need to win. Track your confirmed supporters. Get them to the polls. Upload voted lists every hour. Call everyone who has not voted by 3pm. Polls close at 8pm. Keep going."

## Verification Checklist
- [ ] Priority list loads with P1-P4 tiers
- [ ] File upload accepts CSV and Excel
- [ ] Matching works (name + address)
- [ ] Strike-off view updates after upload
- [ ] Election day command shows pacing
- [ ] Templates CRUD works
- [ ] Turnout percentage calculates correctly
