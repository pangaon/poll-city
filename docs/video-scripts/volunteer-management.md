# Video Walkthrough: Volunteer Management
Duration: ~4 minutes
Feature: Volunteer Management (#9)

## Script

### Scene 1: Opening (0:00 - 0:15)
SCREEN: Dashboard
VOICE: "Your volunteers are the engine of your campaign. Poll City helps you recruit them, schedule them, track their hours, and make sure they keep coming back."

### Scene 2: Volunteer List (0:15 - 0:45)
SCREEN: Click Volunteers in sidebar
VOICE: "Go to Volunteers. You see every volunteer on your team — their name, role, skills, availability, and total hours. The leaderboard shows who is putting in the work."
ACTION: Show the volunteer list

### Scene 3: Adding a Volunteer (0:45 - 1:15)
SCREEN: Quick Capture or Add Volunteer
VOICE: "Add a volunteer with Quick Capture — name, email, phone. They get added to your team and you can assign them to shifts immediately. For self-registration, share your join link or QR code — volunteers sign up themselves."
ACTION: Show join link and QR code

### Scene 4: Creating Shifts (1:15 - 1:50)
SCREEN: Shifts tab
VOICE: "Create canvass shifts. Saturday 10am to 2pm, Oak Street turf, need 4 volunteers. Post it and your team can sign up. Poll City sends reminders automatically."
ACTION: Create a shift with date, time, location, capacity

### Scene 5: Volunteer Groups (1:50 - 2:15)
SCREEN: Groups tab
VOICE: "Organize volunteers into groups — the Saturday Team, the Phone Bank Crew, Neighbourhood Captains. Send messages to a whole group with one click."
ACTION: Show groups and message button

### Scene 6: Check-In and Hours (2:15 - 2:45)
SCREEN: Shift detail with check-in
VOICE: "When a volunteer arrives, check them in. Poll City automatically credits their hours. At the end of the campaign, you know exactly who put in the time."

### Scene 7: Expenses (2:45 - 3:15)
SCREEN: Expenses tab
VOICE: "Volunteers submit expense claims — mileage, supplies, food for the team. Campaign managers approve or reject. Everything is tracked for your financial filing."
ACTION: Show expense submission and approval flow

### Scene 8: Bulk Actions (3:15 - 3:35)
SCREEN: Bulk activate/deactivate
VOICE: "Need to onboard 20 new volunteers after a recruitment event? Bulk activate them. Someone not showing up? Deactivate — clean and accountable."

### Scene 9: Closing (3:35 - 4:00)
SCREEN: Dashboard with volunteer stats
VOICE: "Thank your volunteers within 24 hours of every shift. Feed them. Celebrate the wins publicly. A volunteer who comes back twice will come back ten times. The second shift is the hardest to get."
