# Video Walkthrough: Campaign Analytics
Duration: ~3 minutes
Feature: Campaign Analytics (#15)

## Script

### Scene 1: Opening (0:00 - 0:10)
VOICE: "Data drives decisions. Poll City analytics show you exactly where your campaign stands and where to focus next."

### Scene 2: Campaign Overview (0:10 - 0:40)
SCREEN: Click Analytics in sidebar
VOICE: "The overview dashboard shows your total contacts, support rate, doors knocked, volunteers active, signs deployed, and donation total. All in real time."

### Scene 3: Canvassing Analytics (0:40 - 1:10)
VOICE: "The canvassing tab shows doors per day over the last 30 days. You see your top canvassers and turf completion percentages. If a turf is at 30%, that is where to send people Saturday."

### Scene 4: Supporter Breakdown (1:10 - 1:40)
VOICE: "The supporter tab breaks down support by ward and by poll. You see your conversion funnel — how many contacts moved from unknown to undecided to supporter. Ward 12 is your strongest area. Poll 7 is lagging — focus there."

### Scene 5: Donation Analytics (1:40 - 2:10)
VOICE: "The donation tab shows total raised, average donation, spending vs limit, and monthly trend. You are at 62% of your spending limit with 4 months to go — on track."

### Scene 6: GOTV Analytics (2:10 - 2:40)
VOICE: "The GOTV tab shows how many supporters have voted, your turnout rate, and your P1 through P4 breakdown. On election day this is the only tab that matters."

### Scene 7: Closing (2:40 - 3:00)
VOICE: "Check analytics weekly. Identify what is working. Double down. The campaigns that win are the ones that let data drive their decisions."
